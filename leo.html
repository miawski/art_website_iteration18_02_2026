LEO’S ART

Leo explaining the art:

My works stem from a fascination with untamed nature. Inspired by the patterns of the forest and mythological beings, the motifs move in a borderland between dream and reality, where organic forms grow, flow, and merge together.

Many of the images emerge from dreams and impressions of nature, where hidden faces and creatures can be glimpsed among branches and layers of earth.

BioPattern / Cybersigilism

Leo uses these kind of patterns in tattooing etc.

Avantgarde

I create mythological and dream-like paintings, drawings, and studies where the subconscious and symbolism meet.
