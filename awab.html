Awab

Awab's art moves between symbolism, digital experiments and visual storytelling. The work explores identity, memory and myth through layered images, animated elements and a playful connection between hand-made and digital expression.

Sekhmet Face

This Sekhmet face was created by separating the face and the eyes into two image layers. The base image stays still, while the eye layer is placed directly on top of it. With JavaScript, the eyes can react to the mouse position while still staying inside the eye sockets.
